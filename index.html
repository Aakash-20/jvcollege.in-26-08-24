About Us

The J.V. Institute of Pharmacy, located in Khamona, Taj. Rajura, Dist. Chandrapur, Maharashtra, is a distinguished institution dedicated to advancing pharmaceutical education. Established with a commitment to excellence, the institute aims to be at the forefront of nurturing knowledge and skills in the pharmaceutical sciences.

Pharmaceutical education is a cornerstone of the global healthcare system, and our institute recognizes the pivotal role that pharmacists play in enhancing healthcare and industry. We are dedicated to cultivating this potential through quality education, aiming to produce professionals who can meet the needs of the industry, employment, and business with integrity and excellence.

As we continue to expand, the J.V. Institute of Pharmacy is committed to adopting innovative technologies, methodologies, and disciplines. Our focus is on academic excellence and a versatile approach to education, ensuring that we meet the evolving needs of our students. We support and encourage research and learning, providing our students with the knowledge and skills they need to succeed in their careers.

Vision - Mission

Vision

To be recognized globally for its excellences in pursuit of newer horizons to build self-reliant pharmacist through quality pharmacy education.

Mission

To impact high-quality technical education and training that enable students to acquire in-depth knowledge and expertise in the field of pharmaceutical sciences to center the need of pharmacy profession and society at large.

Objective

To become a global network of premier academic institutions, each committed to nurturing knowledge that fosters happiness, peace, harmony, and prosperity.

Our Facilities

  • Conference Hall

    Our auditorium is a key venue for conferences, workshops, and seminars, equipped with all necessary facilities to support the learning process. It offers a spacious environment that meets the diverse needs of quality education.

  • Library

    The library is at the heart of the J.V. Institute of Pharmacy's mission, as eloquently stated by former President of India Dr. S. Radhakrishnan. Our library is dedicated to serving the needs and requirements of both teachers and students, supporting their reading, study, and research endeavors.

  • Digital Classrooms

    We have Smart Classrooms designed to enhance teaching and learning opportunities.

  • Specialized Laboratory

    Each department at the J.V. Institute of Pharmacy has excellent labs where students can practice and improve their skills. We regularly add new and advanced equipment to our labs, using the latest technology to give our students an advantage.

  • Sport

    The J.V. Institute of Pharmacy campus features diverse sports facilities that enable us to host a variety of events, fostering an active and engaging campus life for students.

  • Campus WiFi

    The J.V. Institute of Pharmacy offers high-speed internet with 24/7 Wi-Fi coverage across the campus areas. This ensures that both students and faculty members can access the internet anywhere on the premises at any time.
